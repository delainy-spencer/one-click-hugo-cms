## 3. Lemon Vinegar Cleaner  

Citrus peels + vinegar = a naturally fresh cleaner that actually works.  

**How to make it:**  
- Fill a mason jar with citrus peels.  
- Cover with white vinegar and let it steep for two weeks.  
- Strain and dilute 1:1 with water.  

Use it on counters, sinks, and floors for a fresh, nontoxic clean.
